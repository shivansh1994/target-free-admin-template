The Power of Connectivity

Pentair's Link2O™ technology is a wireless remote communication system that monitors connected devices, providing the power of constant contact – whether you're home or away, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Link2O continuously monitors connected devices and sends text and/or email changes to your smartphone, tablet or computer to alert you of any changes in operating conditions. Knowledge is power. With Pentair's Link2O technology, you'll have the knowledge of real time status of your home's water systems. With constant connection, the power is yours.

How it Works

Link2O is a cloud based wireless ecosystem that enables 2 way communication between you and your product. The Link2O Gateway device plugs into your home's router. Your product "talks" to the gateway device, which then communicates with your mobile device and/or computer.

Protect Your Home

Whether you are at home, on vacation, at work, or anywhere else in the world, you can communicate with your system and be confident that your home's water systems are functioning correctly.
